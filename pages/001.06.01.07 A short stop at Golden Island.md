- Your supplies are not enough to reach the capital. You have to make a short stop at Golden Island in order to resupply. Golden Island is a small Island to the south-east of the capital. It is a major hub for merchants from all over the world. Trading made the Island rich despite it's extremely small coastline and the fact that there is only one small shop on the whole island. That's where the name Golden Island originates from. It should be a one week trip from your current location to Golden Island. And then just one week more and you are finally back to the capital.
